Dave Carter is a writer, musican, street-performer, radio presenter & full time human being based in Sydney.

In 2013 he released his first self-produced hip-hop record, and performed in 13 cities across Europe & the US, from Rome, Paris and London to New York, Las Vegas and Black Rock City.

Along the way he took punches to the face for his art in Berlin, rap-battled hustlers in Central Park and opened the world’s first improvised musical buffet, the “Artisan Beatery” at the Burning Man Festival in Nevada, serving up fresh beatbox & rhymes made-to-order.

And now he’s home in Sydney, walking and talking the fine line between real and dream.

Dave developed & presents the radio show TrackSuits, fronts 4-piece rocktronica outfit Spectacles and performs poetry, hiphop and improvised music made-to-order in his musical restaurant on the streets & on stages everywhere.
